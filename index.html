About Me

I am currently working at Baidu Inc. I received my Ph.D. from Institute for Interdisciplinary Information Sciences (IIIS), Zhejiang University, headed by Prof. Andrew Yao (姚期智). My advisor is Prof. Chongjie Zhang (张崇洁) and I am the first graduate from Machine Intelligence Group . My research interests include Reinforcement Learning （强化学习） and Model-based Agent（基于模型的智能体）. My thesis is to improve the sample efficiency of the RL Agent via inductive models including object-oriented representation model, plannable world model, and associative memory model.
